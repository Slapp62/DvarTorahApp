ShabbosVorts Account Deletion

Effective date: April 12, 2026

If you have a ShabbosVorts account, you can request deletion directly inside the app.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open Profile.
  2. Tap Delete account.
  3. Confirm deletion.

What Will Be Deleted

  • Your user profile
  • Your writer application records
  • Your submitted reports
  • Your likes
  • Divrei Torah you authored in the app

What May Be Retained

We may retain limited information where necessary for legal compliance, security, fraud prevention, abuse handling, or dispute resolution.

If Deletion Fails

In some cases, account deletion may require a recent login session. If deletion does not complete, sign in again and retry the deletion process.
