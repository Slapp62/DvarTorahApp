Quick Dvar Torah Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 12, 2026

Quick Dvar Torah respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have when using the app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information such as your name, email address, and sign-in details.
  • User content such as Divrei Torah, writer applications, reports, likes, and profile details.
  • Technical information used to operate the app, improve reliability, and protect the service.

2. How We Use Information

  • To create and manage user accounts.
  • To let users browse, write, publish, and manage Divrei Torah.
  • To review writer applications and moderate reported content.
  • To improve app performance, reliability, and safety.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce app policies.

3. User Content and Moderation

If you submit Divrei Torah, applications, or reports, that information may be stored and reviewed by administrators for moderation, safety, and quality purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

Quick Dvar Torah uses third-party services that may process information on our behalf, including:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Cloud Firestore
  • Firebase Storage
  • Brevo, for writer approval and onboarding emails

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only as needed to provide the app’s functionality, work with service providers, protect users, enforce policies, or respond to legal obligations.

6. Data Retention

We keep information for as long as needed to operate the app, maintain moderation records, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes. If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize data as described in our account deletion policy, except where retention is required for legal, security, or abuse-prevention reasons.

7. Your Choices

  • You may sign out of your account.
  • You may delete your account through the app.

8. Children

Quick Dvar Torah is not intended for children under 13 unless explicitly stated otherwise. If we learn that personal information from a child has been collected improperly, we will take reasonable steps to delete it.

9. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information, but no system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after updates means the revised policy will apply.

11. Contact
